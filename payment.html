Payment Options for your Custom Workouts

PayPal

Currently, NC Personal Fitness can only accept payment through PayPal. We are very flexible about payment, and can prorate a plan on a per workout basis if requested. While flexible, we must have a downpayment of at least 50% of the total cost of the plan, prior to the delivery of your first paid workout.

Money Back Guarentee

At NC Fitness, we like to do things right. We stand behind our workouts and their amazing quality, but if you feel otherwise, we will give you 100% of your money back, no questions asked.
